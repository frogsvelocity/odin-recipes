ONIGIRI

Takoyaki are a Japanese street snack that originated in the city of Osaka.
They are little round balls of batter that are slightly crispy on the outside and a bit soft and gooey on the inside,
stuffed with a little nugget of octopus, tempura bits, and green onions.

INGREDIENTS

rice
furikake
nori

DIRECTIONS

STEP 1

Pop your cooked rice into a nice mixing bowl. Add the furikake or rice flavouring and mix through evenly.
Separate the rice into equal portions, big enough to be a large handful each.

STEP 2

If you are hiding some fillings inside, here is where you make an indent, place the ingredients inside
and fold the rice over, then lightly press into a ball.

STEP 3

Using mainly your fingertips while resting the rice on your palm, start to press and squeeze
the rice into a triangular shape, rotating as you go so it's even.

STEP 4

Place a slice of nori on the bottom of the onigiri (the rough side should face the rice) and fold it up towards to the middle of the onigiri.
Serve and Enjoy!
